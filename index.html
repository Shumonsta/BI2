How It Works

01

Information

A group of young programming pups, under the supervision of the mature Foxes-programmers came up with this idea while doing our course tasks. We also were lonely during confinement time and felt an urgency to put our programming fingers into connecting the most vulnerable at this crazy time folks. We believe in a bright future when we are all together and look out for each other.

02

Who is it for?

While parents are busy working, children aka READOPUPS, and wise older generation with plenty energy to spend aka READOFOXES, can gang up here for fun. Reading out loud together, telling stories, doing crosswords, playing chess (why not even musical instrument?!), all this can happen here

03

Activities We Promote

We welcome volunteers to participate in reading out loud and recording the books, to tell their funny or useful first-hand experiences and stories (it is never too late to start your own writing career, you know!), to come up with quizzes or crosswords, or with the joke of the day.

We can share the process of creating our own library of fun and communication, while those who need a bedtime story NOW, can just close their eyes and listen to the chosen favourites.
